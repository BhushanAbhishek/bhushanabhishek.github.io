About Me

Since 2011, I have been designing beautiful graphics for print as well as digital media and now I am specifically into designing User Interface and User Experience. I have loved designing and will always do, as it is my only passion and profession of course.

After joining the Post Graduate course of M.Des from NIFT in 2016, I have been working on User Experience and User Research and at the same time enhancing my visual design skills as well. “M.Des from NIFT is a very balanced course which is making me learn to focus on problems and solutions in recent media trends.” Plus working as a freelance designer for last 6 years has given me a lot of experience not only in design process but also in project management and team work.

And Yes, Simple and Functional Design is what I believe in.

I love admiring things and believe in keen observations. Every single visual I come across, I make it serve as an inspiration for my future designs.
